The *central limit theorem* states that for a random variable $$X$$ such that $$\mathbb{E}X = \mu$$ and $$\mathbb{E}X^2 < \infty$$,

$$
\sqrt(n)(\overline{X} - \mu) \rightarrow \mathcal{N} (0, \mathrm{var} X)
$$

holds.
